Founder & CEO · utilITise · Houston, TX

I build at the intersection of business, technology, and story — turning complex IT into momentum-driving, customer-admired growth engines.

Fifteen years leading $200M+ IT and cybersecurity divisions taught me one thing: the problem with IT is not the technology. It requires constant human attention to work. I started utilITise to fix that — agentic AI that resolves IT issues before anyone notices they exist.

I'm a technologist at heart and a marketer by craft. My philosophy stays consistent: Know us. Like us. Trust us. Pay us.
