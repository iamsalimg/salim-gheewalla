Founder & CEO · utilITise · Houston, TX

I build at the intersection of business, technology, and story — turning complex IT infrastructure into growth engines for the businesses that need it most.

Fifteen years leading $200M+ IT and cybersecurity divisions taught me one thing: the problem with IT is not the technology. It requires constant human attention to work. I started utilITise to change that — building agentic AI that autonomously detects, troubleshoots, and resolves IT issues before anyone notices they exist.
